About FairRentalAI

Rental screening that’s
fair by design.

We built FairRentalAI because finding a tenant should be about fit — not gut feeling. Our platform uses AI to give every applicant a fair, objective evaluation based on what actually matters.

Our mission
Removing bias from
rental decisions.

Traditional rental screening is inconsistent, time-consuming, and often unconsciously biased. We built FairRentalAI to fix that — giving landlords better data and tenants a fairer chance.

⚖️
Fairness first
Every applicant is scored on the same objective criteria — income, employment, rental history, and household fit. We never consider name, photo, ethnicity, or any personal characteristic.
📊
Data-driven decisions
Landlords deserve better than gut instinct. Our AI synthesizes every data point and explains its reasoning, so you can make confident, defensible decisions.
🏠
Better outcomes for everyone
When screening is fair and efficient, landlords find better-matched tenants and tenants get a genuine shot based on their actual qualifications.
🔒
Privacy & security
All documents are encrypted at rest and in transit. Tenant documents are automatically deleted within 30 days of lease signing. We never sell your data.
Technology
Built on world-class
infrastructure.

FairRentalAI is powered by the same enterprise-grade technology used by the world’s leading companies.

🤖
Claude AI (Anthropic)
Our AI scoring engine is powered by Anthropic’s Claude — one of the most capable and safety-focused AI models in the world.
☁️
Cloudflare R2
All tenant documents are stored in Cloudflare’s encrypted, globally distributed object storage. Private by default, never publicly accessible.
💳
Stripe
Payments are processed by Stripe — the same payment infrastructure used by Amazon, Google, and millions of businesses worldwide.
📧
SendGrid (Twilio)
All transactional emails are sent via Twilio SendGrid, ensuring reliable, authenticated delivery to landlords and tenants.
🗄️
PostgreSQL
Your data is stored in a secure, fully-managed PostgreSQL database with automatic backups and enterprise-grade reliability.
🌐
Vercel & Railway
Our platform runs on Vercel’s global edge network and Railway’s managed infrastructure for maximum speed and reliability.
