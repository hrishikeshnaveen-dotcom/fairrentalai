04
AI ranks your applicants
As tenants submit applications, FairRentalAI automatically scores each one and ranks them best to worst. The score is based on income, credit, employment, rental history, household fit, and move-in date.
Scores are 0–100. Higher = better match for your property
Every score includes an AI explanation and strengths/concerns
No name, photo, or personal characteristics are used in scoring
Results update in real time as new applications come in
