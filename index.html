Screening criteria
Every factor that matters.

FairRentalAI scores applicants on objective criteria — never on name, photo, or anything that introduces bias.

Income & ability to pay
Income-to-rent ratio. Ideally 3× the monthly rent.
Credit score
Self-reported credit score weighted alongside other factors.
Employment stability
Employment type and tenure at current role.
Rental history
Years renting, past evictions, and landlord references.
Household fit
A 1-bed is scored differently for a couple vs. a family of 5.
Pets & move-in date
Match applicants to your property's pet policy and timeline.
