Screening criteria
Every factor that matters.
Nothing that doesn't.

FairRentalAI scores applicants on objective, financial, and lifestyle fit criteria — never on name, photo, or anything that introduces bias.

Income & ability to pay
We check the income-to-rent ratio. Ideally 3× the monthly rent for a strong score.
Credit score
Self-reported credit score weighted alongside other factors — not a dealbreaker on its own.
Employment stability
Full-time, self-employed, contract — and how long they've been in their current role.
Rental history
Years renting, past evictions, and landlord references all factor into the score.
Household fit
A 1-bed scored differently for a couple vs. a family of 5. Unit size matters for suitability.
Pets & move-in date
Match applicants to your property's pet policy and your preferred move-in timeline.
