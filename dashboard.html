Frequently asked questions
How does AI scoring work?
Our AI scores applicants based on income, credit score, employment stability, rental history, and household fit. We never consider name, photo, or personal characteristics.
How do I add more than 5 properties?
Your first 5 properties are always free. Each additional property is a one-time $5 charge with no monthly fees.
How do tenants apply?
Each property gets a unique application link. Share it in your listing. Tenants click it and fill out the form with no account needed.
What happens after I approve a tenant?
Initial approval emails the tenant asking for documents. Once uploaded you review and give final approval which sends them a confirmation email.
